Who is Garen?

Garen is a member of the Demacian Crownguard family, joining the military of Demacia at the age of 12 and rising through the ranks to earn a place among the elite Vanguard. Garen has played a pivotal role in Demacia's long lasting war against Noxus and is a popular champion choice among summoners in Summoner's Rift.

In Summoner's Rift, where special summoners select champions to fight in an arena to settle world problems, Garen is picked for being straightforward and beginner-friendly.
